Mythorum Domus
(Home of the Mythical)

As they approached the outskirts of the town, they were greeted by the sight of colourful banners fluttering in the breeze and the sound of cheerful voices drifting on the air. Mythorum Domus was alive with excitement, its streets thronged with villagers who had come out to welcome their heroes home. Cheers erupted as Lyra, Aurelia, and Celeste made their way through the crowded streets, their faces beaming with pride and joy. They were met with hugs, handshakes, and expressions of gratitude from all who crossed their path, their hearts warmed by the outpouring of love and support from their fellow townsfolk. At the heart of the town square, a grand feast awaited them, a testament to the town's appreciation for their bravery and sacrifice. Tables groaned under the weight of delicious food and drink, while musicians played lively tunes that set the mood for the festivities to come. As the sun set on the horizon, casting a warm glow over the town, Lyra, Aurelia, and Celeste took their places at the head of the table, surrounded by their friends, family, and fellow adventurers. It was a moment of pure joy and celebration, a time to reflect on the challenges they had faced and the triumphs they had achieved. And as they raised their glasses in a toast to the future, their hearts filled with hope and excitement for the adventures that lay ahead.
